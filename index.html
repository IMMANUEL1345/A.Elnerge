Every line of code carries a founder's signature.

A. Elnerge Technologies is not named after a place, a trend, or an acronym already in use by a dozen other companies. It is built from the very letters of our founder's name: Immanuel Boanerge Agbemabiase.

Beginning with a final-year project tackling transport logistics, Immanuel saw a universal truth: every industry — from the local retailer to the busy construction project manager — is held back by fragmented, non-intelligent tools.

This sparked the creation of the IBA Suite: a family of Intelligent Business Applications where each product is deeply specialized for African business realities.

"That's Intelligent. That's Business. That's Elnerge."
